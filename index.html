Hi. I'm Wasim Reza.

I have a passion for data, data visualization and storytelling through formed analysis!

Here's a bit about my academic and work experience

Insitute of Business Administration, University of Dhaka

This is where I made lifelong friends and where the professors pushed us hard and inspired us to aim higher.

My rotational program in Bangladesh changed my life and formed the foundation for an international career in Singapore. I worked here for 8 years with roles in coporate banking and trade finance securitization. It was in the last 3 years in Risk Reporting that set the tone for the rest of my career.

I did my MBA when I migrated to Canada. I found it challenging at first but soon got the hang of it. It really helped me assimilate into the Canadian way of things. The experience here gave me the confidence for continuous learning and access to a vast network.

My first role in Canada was as a Reporting Consultant. I had to learn SQL and SAS fairly quickly to migrate the excel/access based reports to the new platform. I was doing my full-time MBA and this role concurrently. I hardly slept!

I love working in my current role in Risk Reporting & Analytics. We started our journey with Power BI and I have had the chance to apply my knowledge of business, data, scripting and data visualization skills (through Power BI) to full effect.


In 2018, supported by a loving wife and resisting the curiosity of my then, one-year old, I passed my Level III exams. It was the hardest of all my academic experiences. I was very fortunate that my wife was always there for me. She got me my BAII Plus several years back.
